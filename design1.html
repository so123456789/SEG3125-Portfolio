Our Story

About ClipZ

Founded in 2019, ClipZ started as a two-chair shop on Bank Street with a simple mission: deliver consistent, high-quality cuts in a comfortable, no-fuss environment.

Our barbers bring years of experience and a passion for the craft. Whether you're coming in for a quick tidy-up or a full grooming session, you'll leave looking sharp.
